Web Development Sandbox

Kathryn's Web Development Sandbox

This repository is used for the small web-based side projects I play around with until they get big enough to warrant their own repository

Soosh Trait Visualizer

Because I have trouble coming up with how to combine sushi dog traits, I made a "dress-up"-game type thing that gives me a "base" to start off with. I plan to eventually move into a separate Sushidog repository than here.
Personality Generator

Random personality traits generator to help me come up with personalities for characters. Was not able to get an API call to a dictionary to fetch word definitions, so instead I made a .js file with a 2D array of words and definitions.
NES-Style Sprite Generator

I modified a pre-existing sprite generator as an exercise in character design for me. I click the generate button, and I draw what I think the sprite empbodies. Inspired by old NES graphics that didn't have a lot of detail to them, while full-page artwork in magazines filled in the design details (especially the arcade game Ladybug, pictured above).
Data Miners

This was a sketch for a potential video game idea based off the mining mechanic in Pokemon Diamond/Pearl/Platinum. The goal is to mine the rocks to retrieve treasures before the walls collapse. Planned to expand to make trickier treasures, possible user aids, and make cyber-themed - hence the title.
Town of Salem Tracker

An outdated role-tracker for the online Mafia-esque community game, "Town of Salem".
RPG Stat Sheet Template

A template I found online and saved into here. I don't know where the source is, so keeping this here for posterity I guess.
