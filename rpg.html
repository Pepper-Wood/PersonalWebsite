Background

Garrus grew up on the Turian homeworld of Palaven. After doing his mandatory service in the Turian Military, Garrus joined Citadel Security(C-Sec) and was assigned to the Saren Arterius case in 2183 and helped Shepard defeat Saren and prevent the first Reaper invasion. He joined the crew on the Normandy until it was destroyed by Collectors. He was unable to return to the red-tape of C-Sec and started a mercenary group on Omega, operating under the codename "Archangel". After stopping the Collectors with Commander Shepard, Garrus went back to the Turian military to attempt to convince them to prepare for the Reaper invasion. Though the inevitable invasion hit Palaven hard, Garrus's efforts helped ensure the survival of the Turian race. He once again joined up with the SR2-Normandy to help defeat the Reapers once and for all.
